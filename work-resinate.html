Artisan Keycaps Showcase

From January 8 to April 17, 2022, a local exhibition titled "Resinate" featuring Singaporean artisan keycap makers was held at the Main Street Commissary, situated at 81 Rowell Rd, Singapore. This café is owned by the local agency PROTOCOL, and has previously exhibited works by local photographers, tattoo artists, and writers.

The Resinate exhibition, initiated by supbryguy from Kin Caps, is a collaborative effort among various Singaporean artisan makers. Its primary objective is to showcase the locally crafted artisan keycaps. By being hosted in a public café, the exhibition also aims to introduce artisan keycaps and the wider mechanical keyboard hobby to a new audience. Although there are some basic display guidelines, the artists have the freedom to decide on their own keycap presentation, display props, and personal expression.
